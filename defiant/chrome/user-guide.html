User Interface

Extension Popup

The Extension Popup is optimized for quick user interactions and consists of the following interface elements:

  1. The Trust Level range slider allows to quickly modify the level of trust for the currently opened website in the active Tab.
  2. The Confirm button allows to apply the changed trust levels which leads to a Tab reload after the button is pressed.
  3. The Options button allows to open the Defiant Extension's Options Page in a new Tab.
  4. The Open in Incognito Window button allows to open the currently visited website in a temporary Incognito window.

Trust Levels

The Defiant Browser Extension works on the concept of trust levels. By default, no website is trusted. You (as the user) are in charge of which website is to be trusted.

Trust Level: Zero

This trust level is best for Static Websites. It is the defaulted trust level. No persistent tracking data of any kind can be set by the Website.

  • Enable Media from first-party or second-party domains.
  • Enable Stylesheets from first-party and second-party domains or known CDNs.

Trust Level: Alpha

This trust level is best for Interactive Websites. It allows dynamic content while trying to preserve your privacy as good as possible. Persistent tracking data is limited to the current Browsing Session, and the lifetime of Cookies is modified so that they are cleared automatically when the Browser is closed.

  • Enable Media from first-party or second-party domains or known CDNs.
  • Enable Stylesheets from first-party and second-party domains or known CDNs.
  • Enable JavaScript from first-party and second-party domains or known CDNs.
  • Enable Cookies from first-party or second-party domains.
  • Limit Cookies to the current browsing session.

Trust Level: Beta

This trust level is best for Web Apps that require third-party Authentication Services or Login Providers. Persistent data is permanently stored and won't be cleared by the Defiant Browser Extension.

  • Enable Media from first-party or second-party domains or known CDNs.
  • Enable Stylesheets from first-party and second-party domains or known CDNs.
  • Enable JavaScript from first-party and second-party domains or known CDNs.
  • Enable Cookies from first-party and second-party domains or known CDNs.
  • Enable Link Prefetching from first-party and second-party domains or known CDNs.
  • Enable Location Redirects from first-party and second-party domains or known CDNs.
  • Enable Frames from first-party and second-party domains.

Trust Level: Gamma

This trust level is best for Social Media Apps that require a lot of tracking mechanisms that do cross-domain communication. Persistent data is permanently stored and won't be cleared by the Defiant Browser Extension.
Use this Trust Level only when you are absolutely sure about the consequences of sharing your identity and fingerprint.

  • Enable Media from all domains.
  • Enable Stylesheets from all domains.
  • Enable JavaScript from all domains.
  • Enable Cookies from all domains.
  • Enable Link Prefetching all domains.
  • Enable Location Redirects from all domains.
  • Enable Frames from all domains.
